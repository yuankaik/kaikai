### B. Reading Comprehension — Multiple Choice 8%

**The Importance of Trees**

Trees are very important to our life. They give us oxygen to breathe. They also give us wood, fruit and many other things. Trees can make our cities more beautiful and keep the air clean.

But people cut down millions of trees every year. This is very harmful to our environment. Without enough trees, the air becomes dirty. Many animals lose their homes. The weather also becomes worse.

We should plant more trees and protect the forests. Everyone can do something to help. We can use less paper. We can recycle paper products. We can also join tree-planting activities. If everyone does a little, our world will become greener and better.

(   ) 1. What do trees give us to breathe?
A. Wood  B. Oxygen  C. Fruit  D. Water

(   ) 2. What happens when people cut down too many trees?
A. The air becomes cleaner.  B. Animals find new homes.
C. The air becomes dirty.  D. The weather becomes better.

(   ) 3. How can we help protect trees?
A. Use more paper.  B. Cut down more trees.
C. Recycle paper products.  D. Ignore the problem.

(   ) 4. What is the main idea of this passage?
A. Trees give us fruit.  B. People cut down too many trees.
C. We should protect trees for a better world.  D. The weather is getting worse.

### C. Reading Comprehension — Answer Questions 7%

Dear Diary,

Today was a special day. It was our school's English Festival. Students from different grades performed on the stage. Some sang English songs. Some told English stories. Some even performed short English plays.

My class performed a play called "The Little Red Riding Hood". I played the wolf! At first, I was a little nervous. But when I saw my classmates smiling at me, I felt much better. I tried my best to act well. In the end, our play won the first prize! We were all so excited.

After the performances, we had a big English quiz. I answered five questions correctly and got a small gift — an English storybook. I will read it this weekend!

I really enjoyed the English Festival. I hope we can have more activities like this!

1. What special day was it today? ______
2. What play did the writer's class perform? ______
3. What role did the writer play? ______
4. What prize did the play win? ______
5. What gift did the writer get from the English quiz? ______
