### 四、选择填空（30分）

(   ) 1. —______ are you? —I am fine. Thank you.
A. What  B. How  C. Who

(   ) 2. —How many ______? —Five pears.
A. a pear  B. pear  C. pears

(   ) 3. Is he ______ father?
A. your  B. you  C. my

(   ) 4. —______ these your books? —Yes, they are.
A. Is  B. are  C. Are

(   ) 5. It is ______ orange.
A. a  B. an  C. /

(   ) 6. —Who is she? —______.
A. He's my father.  B. She's my mother.

(   ) 7. 你看见新来的老师，好像叫王老师，可以问：______
A. Are you Miss Wang?  B. You are Miss Wang.

(   ) 8. Look ______ the blackboard, please.
A. at  B. on  C. in

(   ) 9. —What colour is it? —It's ______.
A. a red  B. red  C. the red

(   ) 10. I ______ a boy. You ______ a girl.
A. am; is  B. is; are  C. am; are

(   ) 11. —Can you swim? —Yes, I ______.
A. can  B. can't  C. am

(   ) 12. This is ______ school. It's big.
A. we  B. us  C. our

(   ) 13. There ______ a library in our school.
A. is  B. are  C. am

(   ) 14. I like ______ basketball.
A. play  B. playing  C. plays

(   ) 15. —______ is the weather today? —It's sunny.
A. What  B. How  C. Who
