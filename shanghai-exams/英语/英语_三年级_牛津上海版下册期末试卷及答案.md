### 七、Reading comprehension（阅读理解，判断正误，用T/F表示） 10%

**A Day in the Park**

It is Sunday today. The weather is fine. Tom and his sister Lily go to the park. There are many people in the park. Some old people are walking. Some children are flying kites. The kites are colourful and beautiful. Tom wants to fly a kite too. He buys a red kite. The kite flies high in the sky. Lily is very happy. She likes watching the kites. They have a good time in the park.

(   ) 1. It is Saturday today.
(   ) 2. Tom and Lily go to the zoo.
(   ) 3. Some old people are walking in the park.
(   ) 4. Tom buys a blue kite.
(   ) 5. Lily likes watching the kites.

**Four Seasons**

There are four seasons in a year: spring, summer, autumn and winter. In spring, it is warm. The trees are green. Flowers bloom everywhere. People like to fly kites in spring. In summer, it is hot. People like to swim and eat ice cream. In autumn, it is cool. Leaves turn yellow and fall from the trees. Farmers are busy in autumn. In winter, it is cold. It often snows. Children like to make snowmen.

(   ) 1. There are five seasons in a year.
(   ) 2. It is warm in spring.
(   ) 3. People like to fly kites in summer.
(   ) 4. Leaves turn yellow in autumn.
(   ) 5. Children like to make snowmen in winter.
