# 上海小学英语 - 四年级 Module 2 单元测验
## Oxford Shanghai Edition 4A Module 2 Me, my family and friends

**时间：45分钟  满分：100分**

### Part 1 Listening 40分

**I. Listen and choose（听音选择）10分**
1. A. father  B. mother  C. brother
2. A. doctor  B. teacher  C. nurse
3. A. short  B. tall  C. thin
4. A. friend  B. family  C. classmate
5. A. dance  B. sing  C. draw

**II. Listen and fill（听音填空）10分**
My name is Tom. I have a happy (1)______. My father is a (2)______. He is tall and (3)______. My mother is a (4)______. She can (5)______ well.

**III. Listen and judge T/F（听音判断）10分**
1. Peter is ten years old. (    )
2. His hair is short. (    )
3. He can play football. (    )
4. He has a sister. (    )
5. His sister can dance. (    )

**IV. Listen and answer（听音回答）10分**
1. How many people are there in Tom's family?
2. What does Tom's father do?

### Part 2 Written 60分

**I. Choose the different sound（找出发音不同的一项）10分**
1. A. name  B. cake  C. cat  D. make
2. A. bike  B. fish  C. like  D. rice
3. A. nose  B. note  C. not  D. home

**II. Multiple choice（选择）15分**
1. My father (    ) a teacher.
   A. is  B. are  C. am
2. — (    ) is she? — She is my sister.
   A. What  B. Who  C. How
3. He (    ) short hair and big eyes.
   A. have  B. has  C. is

**III. Fill in blanks（用所给词适当形式填空）15分**
1. My mother can (    )(dance) very well.
2. I (    )(have) a good friend.
3. She (    )(like) reading books.

**IV. Reading（阅读判断 T/F）20分**
Hello, I'm Alice. I have a good friend. Her name is Kitty. She is tall and thin. She has long hair and big eyes. She can sing and dance. We often play together after school. I like her very much.
1. Alice's friend is Kitty. (    )
2. Kitty is short. (    )
3. Kitty has short hair. (    )
4. Kitty can sing and dance. (    )
5. They play together after school. (    )
