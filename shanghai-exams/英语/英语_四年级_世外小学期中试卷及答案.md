## Part 3 Reading（阅读部分） 25%

### A. Read and choose（阅读选择） 10%

Hi, I'm David. I'm a student at Shanghai World Foreign Language Primary School. I'm in Class 3, Grade 4. We have four classes in the morning and two in the afternoon. My favourite day is Tuesday because we have English, Art and PE on that day.

Our school is very beautiful. There is a big library with thousands of books. We can borrow three books each time. There is also a computer room, a music room and an art room. The playground is big. We can play football and basketball there.

Our teachers are very kind and helpful. Miss Li is our English teacher. She speaks English very well. She often tells us interesting stories and plays games with us. Mr Wang is our maths teacher. He is strict but fair. We all respect him.

I love my school very much!

(   ) 1. David is in Grade ______.
A. 3  B. 4  C. 5  D. 6

(   ) 2. David's favourite day is ______ because he has English, Art and PE.
A. Monday  B. Tuesday  C. Wednesday  D. Thursday

(   ) 3. Students can borrow ______ books each time from the library.
A. two  B. three  C. four  D. five

(   ) 4. Miss Li is David's ______ teacher.
A. maths  B. Chinese  C. English  D. music

(   ) 5. Mr Wang is described as ______.
A. strict but fair  B. kind and helpful  C. funny  D. young

### B. Read and judge T/F（阅读判断） 8%

Shanghai is a great city. It is in the east of China. It is one of the biggest cities in the world. There are more than 24 million people living here.

Shanghai has many famous places. The Bund is a very popular place. You can see both old and new buildings there. The Oriental Pearl TV Tower is 468 metres tall. People can go up to the top and see the whole city.

Shanghai is also famous for its food. Xiaolongbao is a kind of steamed bun with soup inside. It is very delicious. Many people from other countries also like it.

(   ) 1. Shanghai is in the west of China.
(   ) 2. There are more than 24 million people in Shanghai.
(   ) 3. The Bund has only old buildings.
(   ) 4. The Oriental Pearl TV Tower is 468 metres tall.
(   ) 5. Xiaolongbao is a kind of steamed bun without soup.

### C. Read and answer（阅读回答） 7%

My name is Lily. I have a pet dog. His name is Coco. He is three years old. He has white and brown fur. He has big eyes and a small nose. He is very cute.

Coco is very clever. He can do many tricks. He can sit, stand and shake hands. Every morning, he wakes me up at 7:00. He likes to play with a ball. After school, I often take him for a walk in the park. He always runs and jumps happily.

Coco is my best friend. When I am sad, he stays with me. When I am happy, he is happy too. I love Coco very much.

2. What colour is Coco's fur? ______
3. What tricks can Coco do? (Name at least two) ______
4. When does Coco wake Lily up? ______
5. Where does Lily take Coco for a walk? ______
