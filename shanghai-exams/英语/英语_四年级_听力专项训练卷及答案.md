## Part 4 听短文，选择正确答案（Listen and choose） 20%

**Passage A — My School Day**

(   ) 1. What time does the speaker get up?
A. At 6:30  B. At 7:00  C. At 7:30

(   ) 2. How does the speaker go to school?
A. By bus  B. On foot  C. By underground

(   ) 3. What does the speaker have for breakfast?
A. Bread and milk  B. Noodles  C. Rice

(   ) 4. How many lessons does the speaker have in the morning?
A. Three  B. Four  C. Five

(   ) 5. What does the speaker like best?
A. English  B. PE  C. Art

**Passage B — A Trip to the Zoo**

(   ) 6. When did they go to the zoo?
A. Last Saturday  B. Last Sunday  C. Yesterday

(   ) 7. How did they go there?
A. By car  B. By bus  C. By taxi

(   ) 8. What animals did they see first?
A. Monkeys  B. Pandas  C. Elephants

(   ) 9. How many pandas did they see?
A. One  B. Two  C. Three

(   ) 10. What did they do at noon?
A. Had lunch  B. Went home  C. Took photos
