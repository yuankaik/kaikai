(   ) 6. A. cheap  B. expensive  C. price  D. beautiful

### 七、Choose the best answer（选择最恰当的答案） 12%

(   ) 1. There ______ a film tomorrow evening.
A. is  B. will be  C. will have

(   ) 2. —______ do you go to the library? —Twice a week.
A. How long  B. How often  C. How far

(   ) 3. My brother is ______ than me, but I run ______ than him.
A. tall; fast  B. taller; faster  C. taller; fast

(   ) 4. She ______ to the supermarket yesterday.
A. go  B. goes  C. went

(   ) 5. —______ is the weather like in spring? —It's warm.
A. How  B. What  C. Which

(   ) 6. You'd better ______ too much TV. It's bad for your eyes.
A. not watch  B. not to watch  C. don't watch

(   ) 7. There are ______ students in our class, twenty boys and twenty-two girls.
A. forty  B. forty-two  C. forty-two

(   ) 8. —Whose pen is this? Is it ______? —No, it's not ______. It's Tom's.
A. your; my  B. yours; mine  C. you; me

(   ) 9. Would you like ______ some tea?
A. have  B. to have  C. having

(   ) 10. —______ did you go on your holiday? —I went to Beijing.
A. Where  B. What  C. How

(   ) 11. Look! The children ______ happily in the playground.
A. play  B. are playing  C. played

(   ) 12. —How much ______ the vegetables? —They ______ 12 yuan.
A. is; is  B. are; are  C. are; is

### 八、Fill in the blanks（用所给词的适当形式填空） 7%

1. Listen! The girl _______ (cry) in the next room.
2. He _______ (not do) his homework last night.
3. May is the _______ (five) month of the year.
4. My sister _______ (have) a new dress. It's beautiful.
5. How many _______ (tomato) do you want?
6. She enjoys _______ (dance) very much.
7. I _______ (visit) my grandparents next Sunday.

### 九、Rewrite the sentences（按要求改写句子） 5%

1. I went to the park yesterday. (改为一般疑问句)
   → _______ you _______ to the park yesterday?

2. She does her homework at 7:00. (对划线部分提问)
   → _______ _______ does she do her homework?

3. There are some flowers in the garden. (改为否定句)
   → There _______ _______ flowers in the garden.

4. Tom is tallest in his class. (改为同义句)
   → Tom is _______ _______ any other student in his class.

5. The coat is 150 yuan. (对划线部分提问)
   → _______ _______ is the coat?

## Part 3 Reading Comprehension（阅读理解） 20%

### 十、Read and judge（阅读短文，判断正误，用T/F表示） 10%

**Passage A: My Weekend**

Last weekend, I had a busy but happy time. On Saturday morning, I did my homework. In the afternoon, I went to the park with my friends. We flew kites. My kite was a butterfly. It was very beautiful. In the evening, I watched a film with my parents. It was about a funny monkey. On Sunday, I visited my grandparents. They live in a small village. Grandma cooked delicious food for us. I helped Grandpa water the flowers in the garden. I had a wonderful weekend.

(   ) 1. I did my homework on Saturday morning.
(   ) 2. I went to the park with my parents on Saturday afternoon.
(   ) 3. My kite was a butterfly.
(   ) 4. I watched a film about a funny cat on Saturday evening.
(   ) 5. Grandpa and I watered the flowers in the garden on Sunday.

**Passage B: Protect the Environment**

Our environment is in danger. The air is dirty. The water is polluted. Many animals are losing their homes. We should do something to protect our environment. We can plant more trees. Trees make the air clean. We can use fewer plastic bags. We can bring our own bags when shopping. We can save water by turning off the tap when brushing teeth. We can also recycle paper, glass and plastic. If everyone helps, our planet will be a better place to live.

(   ) 1. The environment is getting better and better.
(   ) 2. Planting trees can make the air clean.
(   ) 3. We should use more plastic bags.
(   ) 4. We can save water when brushing teeth.
(   ) 5. If everyone helps, our planet will be better.

### 十一、Read and choose（阅读短文，选择正确答案） 10%

**Passage C: A Letter from Beijing**

Dear Alice,

How are you? I'm having a great time in Beijing. I came here with my parents last Monday. We visited many places. On Tuesday, we went to the Great Wall. It's very long and great. I took many photos. On Wednesday, we went to the Palace Museum. There were so many beautiful things to see. Yesterday, we went to the Summer Palace. We rowed a boat on the lake. It was fun. Tomorrow we will go to Tian'anmen Square. Beijing is a wonderful city. I love it. I will show you the photos when I come back.

Yours,
Kitty

(   ) 1. Kitty is in ______ now.
A. Shanghai  B. Beijing  C. Nanjing

(   ) 2. Kitty went to the Great Wall on ______.
A. Monday  B. Tuesday  C. Wednesday

(   ) 3. What did Kitty do at the Summer Palace?
A. She took many photos.  B. She rowed a boat.  C. She climbed a mountain.

(   ) 4. Where will Kitty go tomorrow?
A. The Great Wall.  B. The Palace Museum.  C. Tian'anmen Square.

(   ) 5. Kitty thinks Beijing is ______.
A. terrible  B. wonderful  C. boring

**Passage D: My School**

My school is not very big, but it is very beautiful. There are many trees and flowers in it. In spring, the flowers bloom and the trees turn green. It is very nice. We have a big playground. We play sports and games there. Our classroom is on the second floor. It is clean and bright. There is a computer in our classroom. We have twenty computers in the computer room. We have a library too. There are many books in it. I like reading in the library after class. I love my school very much.

(   ) 1. The school is ______.
A. very big  B. not very big  C. very small

(   ) 2. The playground is ______.
A. small  B. big  C. not big

(   ) 3. The classroom is on the ______ floor.
A. first  B. second  C. third

(   ) 4. How many computers are there in the computer room?
A. 1  B. 10  C. 20

(   ) 5. What does the writer like doing after class?
A. Playing sports.  B. Reading in the library.  C. Playing computer games.
