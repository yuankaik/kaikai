# 上海小学英语 - 三年级 期末模拟考
## Oxford Shanghai Edition 3A Final Mock Exam

**时间：50分钟  满分：100分**

### Part 1 Listening 40分

**I. Listen and choose the word（听音选词）8分**
1. A. classroom  B. library  C. playground
2. A. window  B. door  C. blackboard
3. A. schoolbag  B. pencil  C. ruler
4. A. teacher  B. student  C. friend

**II. Listen and choose the sentence（听音选句）8分**
1. A. Open the door.  B. Close the window.  C. Clean the blackboard.
2. A. This is my friend.  B. This is my teacher.  C. This is my mother.

**III. Listen and judge T/F（听音判断）12分**
1. The classroom is big. (    )
2. There are 40 students. (    )
3. Miss Li is the teacher. (    )
4. The desks are new. (    )

**IV. Listen and fill（听音填空）12分**
My (1)______ is big. There are many (2)______ and chairs. The (3)______ is on the wall. We have an English class on (4)______.

### Part 2 Written 60分

**I. Write the letters（写出相邻字母）8分**
1. ___ H ___  2. ___ q ___  3. ___ X ___

**II. Choose（选择）15分**
1. — (    ) is she? — She's my sister.
   A. What  B. Who  C. How
2. I (    ) a new schoolbag.
   A. have  B. has  C. am
3. — How many (    )? — Ten.
   A. book  B. books  C. a book

**III. Fill in blanks（适当形式填空）12分**
1. She (    )(have) long hair.
2. I can (    )(see) a bird.
3. Let's (    )(go) to school.

**IV. Read and answer（阅读回答）15分**
My name is Tom. I am nine years old. I am a student. I have a new schoolbag. It is blue and white. There are three books, two pencils, and a ruler in it. I like my schoolbag very much.
1. How old is Tom?
2. What colour is the schoolbag?
3. How many books are in the schoolbag?

**V. Write about your school（写写你的学校）10分**
（至少3句话）
