## Part A 阅读理解（Reading Comprehension） 60%

### Passage 1 — A Day at the Park (10%)

Last Sunday, my family went to the Century Park. The weather was sunny and warm. There were many people in the park. Some were flying kites. Some were having a picnic on the grass. My father took many photos with his new camera. My mother sat on the bench and read a book. My little brother played with a toy car. I rode my bike around the lake. We stayed in the park for three hours. We all had a wonderful time.

**Choose the best answer:**

(   ) 1. When did the family go to Century Park?
A. Last Saturday  B. Last Sunday  C. Yesterday

(   ) 2. What was the weather like?
A. Rainy  B. Cloudy  C. Sunny and warm

(   ) 3. What did the father do in the park?
A. He read a book.  B. He took photos.  C. He flew a kite.

(   ) 4. What did the mother do?
A. She flew a kite.  B. She had a picnic.  C. She read a book.

(   ) 5. How long did they stay in the park?
A. Two hours  B. Three hours  C. Four hours

### Passage 2 — My Best Friend (10%)

My best friend is Alice. She is twelve years old. She studies at Shanghai Fushan Foreign Language Primary School. She is tall and thin. She has big eyes and long black hair. She is good at English and she can speak it very well. She also likes singing and dancing. After school, we often do homework together. On weekends, we sometimes go to the library or the cinema. She wants to be an English teacher when she grows up. I think she will be a wonderful teacher.

**Judge the sentences (T/F):**

(   ) 1. Alice is eleven years old.
(   ) 2. Alice studies at Fushan Foreign Language Primary School.
(   ) 3. Alice has small eyes and short hair.
(   ) 4. Alice is good at English.
(   ) 5. Alice wants to be a doctor.

### Passage 3 — The Four Seasons in Shanghai (15%)

There are four seasons in Shanghai. They are spring, summer, autumn and winter.

Spring is from March to May. It is warm. Trees turn green and flowers come out. Sometimes it rains. I like spring because I can fly kites.

Summer is from June to August. It is very hot. The temperature is usually above 30°C. Many people like to go swimming. I like eating ice-cream and watermelons in summer.

Autumn is from September to November. It is cool and dry. The sky is blue and the leaves turn yellow and red. It is the best season for travelling. I like autumn because I can eat many fruits.

Winter is from December to February. It is cold. Sometimes it snows, but not very often. I like winter because I can wear warm clothes and have hot pot with my family.

**Answer the questions:**

1. How many seasons are there in Shanghai? ______
2. When is spring in Shanghai? ______
3. What's the weather like in summer? ______
4. Why does the writer like autumn? ______
5. What does the writer like to do in winter? ______
6. Which season is the best for travelling? ______
7. Does it often snow in Shanghai in winter? ______

### Passage 4 — A Visit to the Science Museum (15%)

Yesterday was Saturday. It was a big day for our class. We went to the Shanghai Science and Technology Museum. We went there by school bus at 8:30 in the morning.

First, we went to the Animal World. We saw many animals there. Some were real and some were models. We learned that the blue whale is the biggest animal in the world.

Then we went to the Robot World. We saw many different robots. They could play music, dance and even play chess. I played chess with a robot and I lost! It was so smart.

After lunch, we went to the Space World. We learned about the solar system and watched a 3D film about space travel. It was amazing. We felt like we were really in space.

We came back to school at 4:00 in the afternoon. Everyone was tired but happy. We learned so many things.

**Choose the best answer:**

(   ) 1. When did the students go to the Science Museum?
A. Last Sunday  B. Last Saturday  C. Last Friday

(   ) 2. How did they go there?
A. By underground  B. By taxi  C. By school bus

(   ) 3. What's the biggest animal in the world?
A. The elephant  B. The blue whale  C. The shark

(   ) 4. What did the writer do with a robot?
A. Danced  B. Sang  C. Played chess

(   ) 5. How did the writer feel about the 3D film?
A. Amazing  B. Boring  C. Scary

### Passage 5 — A Letter to a Pen Friend (10%)

Dear Jenny,

My name is Wang Ming. I'm eleven years old. I live in Shanghai, China. I study at Shanghai World Foreign Language Primary School. I'm in Grade 4.

I have a happy family. There are four people in my family: my father, my mother, my grandfather and me. My father is a doctor. My mother is a teacher. They both work very hard.

I like playing basketball and reading books. My favourite book is Harry Potter. I also like English. I hope to visit England one day. Can you tell me about your school and your hobbies?

Please write back soon.

Best wishes,
Wang Ming

**Answer the questions:**

1. How old is Wang Ming? ______
2. Which school does Wang Ming study at? ______
3. How many people are there in Wang Ming's family? ______
4. What's Wang Ming's mother's job? ______
5. What are Wang Ming's hobbies? ______

## Part B 完形填空（Cloze Test） 40%

### Cloze 2 (20%)

We visited many places in Beijing. The first day, we went to Tian'anmen Square. It was very (3) ______. There were thousands of people there. Then we visited the Forbidden City. It was so big (4) ______ we walked for a long time.

The second day, we went to (5) ______ Great Wall. It is one of the seven wonders of the world. We climbed up and (6) ______ many photos. The view from the top was (7) ______. I felt very proud because I could climb to the top.

We also ate Beijing roast duck. It was the (8) ______ food I have ever eaten. We bought some souvenirs (9) ______ our friends. We had a great (10) ______ there and I hope to visit Beijing again.

## 参考答案

### Part A 阅读理解

**Passage 1:** 1-5: B C B C B

**Passage 2:** 1. F  2. T  3. F  4. T  5. F

**Passage 3:**
1. Four.
2. From March to May.
3. It's very hot. (The temperature is usually above 30°C.)
4. Because he/she can eat many fruits.
5. He/She likes to wear warm clothes and have hot pot with family.
6. Autumn.
7. No, it doesn't snow very often.

**Passage 4:** 1-5: B C B C A

**Passage 5:**
1. Eleven years old.
2. Shanghai World Foreign Language Primary School.
3. Four people.
4. She is a teacher.
5. Playing basketball and reading books.
