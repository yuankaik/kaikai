### II. 用所给词适当形式填空（每题2分，共20分）

5. The lion (    )(become) happy at last.

7. He always (    )(hit) the ball hard.
8. They (    )(have) a party next week.
9. I want (    )(be) a teacher.
10. Let's (    )(go) shopping.

### III. 单项选择（每题2分，共20分）
1. You (    ) play football on the road.
   A. must  B. mustn't  C. can
2. There (    ) any milk in the glass.
   A. isn't  B. aren't  C. is
3. (    ) did you go last Sunday? — I went to the zoo.
   A. What  B. Where  C. When
4. He drinks (    ) water every day.
   A. a little  B. a few  C. many
5. — (    ) will you stay there? — For two weeks.
   A. How long  B. How often  C. How far

### V. 阅读理解（15分）
Good habits are important. We should get up early and go to bed early. We should brush our teeth in the morning and before bedtime. We should eat healthy food like vegetables and fruit. We shouldn't eat too much sweet food. We should also do exercise every day. Good habits make us healthy and happy.

1. Are good habits important?
2. When should we brush our teeth?
3. What food should we eat?
4. What shouldn't we eat too much?
5. Do you have any good habits? Write one.
