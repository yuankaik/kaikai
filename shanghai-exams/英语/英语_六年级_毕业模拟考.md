# 上海小学英语 - 六年级 毕业模拟考
## Oxford Shanghai Edition 6B Graduation Mock

**时间：60分钟  满分：100分**

### Part 1 Listening 30分

**I. Listen and choose（听音选择）6分**
1. A. summer  B. winter  C. autumn
2. A. travel  B. study  C. work
3. A. museum  B. cinema  C. zoo

**II. Listen and choose answer（听问句选答）8分**
1. A. By plane.  B. Next week.  C. With my family.
2. A. Yes, I did.  B. Yes, I do.  C. Yes, I am.

**III. Listen and fill（听音填空）8分**
Summer holiday is coming. I'm going to (1)______. I will go there by (2)______. I will stay there for (3)______ days. I'm very (4)______.

**IV. Listen and judge T/F（听音判断）8分**
1. Tom went to Beijing last summer. (    )
2. He went there by train. (    )
3. He visited the Great Wall. (    )
4. He didn't like the trip. (    )

### Part 2 Written 70分

**I. Choose different sound（找不同发音）6分**
1. A. cake  B. cat  C. make  D. lake
2. A. bike  B. rice  C. fish  D. like

**II. Multiple choice（选择）20分**
1. I (    ) to Shanghai last year.
   A. go  B. went  C. going
2. — (    ) will you go? — Next Monday.
   A. What  B. When  C. Where
3. There (    ) a lot of people in the park yesterday.
   A. is  B. was  C. were

**III. Fill in the blanks（用动词适当形式填空）16分**
1. He (    )(visit) his grandparents last weekend.
2. They (    )(be) happy yesterday.
3. She (    )(not go) to school last Monday.
4. — (    ) you (    )(have) a good time? — Yes, I did.

**IV. Cloze test（完形填空）12分**
Last summer, I (1)___ to Hainan with my parents. We (2)___ there by plane. The weather was hot and (3)___. We swam in the sea and (4)___ many beautiful fish. It was a (5)___ trip. I want to go there (6)___.
1. A. go  B. went  C. going
2. A. go  B. went  C. going
3. A. sunny  B. rainy  C. windy
4. A. see  B. saw  C. seeing
5. A. great  B. bad  C. boring
6. A. too  B. also  C. again

**V. Reading（阅读理解）16分**
Shanghai is a modern city. It's in the east of China. There are many tall buildings. The Oriental Pearl TV Tower is very famous. Many tourists visit Shanghai every year. They like shopping on Nanjing Road and walking along the Bund. The food in Shanghai is also delicious. You can try xiaolongbao and shengjian.
1. Where is Shanghai?
2. What is the Oriental Pearl TV Tower?
3. What do tourists like doing in Shanghai?
4. Can you name two kinds of Shanghai food from the text?
