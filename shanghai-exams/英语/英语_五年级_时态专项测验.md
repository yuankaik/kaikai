# 上海小学英语 - 五年级 时态专项测验
## 一般现在时 vs 现在进行时 vs 一般过去时

**时间：40分钟  满分：100分**

### I. 用所给动词的适当形式填空（每题2分，共30分）
1. She usually (    )(get) up at 7:00.
2. Look! The boys (    )(play) football.
3. I (    )(visit) my grandma last Sunday.
4. He (    )(not like) swimming.
5. — What are you doing? — I (    )(read) a book.
6. They (    )(go) to the park yesterday.
7. My father (    )(watch) TV every evening.
8. Listen! Someone (    )(sing) in the room.
9. We (    )(have) a party last month.
10. She (    )(not go) to school on Sundays.
11. — Where is Tom? — He (    )(run) on the playground.
12. I (    )(be) born in Shanghai.
13. The earth (    )(go) around the sun.
14. — (    ) you (    )(do) homework now?
15. He (    )(not come) to school yesterday.

### II. 按要求改写句子（每题4分，共20分）
1. She reads English every morning.（改为否定句）
2. They are cleaning the classroom.（改为一般疑问句）
3. He went to Beijing last week.（改为否定句）
4. I play football after school.（用now改写）
5. She does her homework at home.（改为一般过去时）

### III. 选择（每题2分，共20分）
1. — (    ) does your father go to work? — By bus.
   A. What  B. How  C. When
2. Tom (    ) his teeth every morning.
   A. brush  B. brushes  C. brushing
3. — Where (    ) you go yesterday? — I went to the library.
   A. do  B. did  C. are
4. My mother (    ) dinner now.
   A. cooks  B. is cooking  C. cooked

### IV. 改错（每题3分，共15分）
1. He go to school at 7:30 every day.
2. They are play football now.
3. I didn't went there yesterday.
4. She don't like apples.
5. Look! The bird fly in the sky.

### V. 用正确时态写5句话描述你的周末（15分）
