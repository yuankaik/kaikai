## Part 3 Reading Comprehension 30%

### A. Read and choose 10%

Most American families are smaller than the families in other countries. Most American families have one or two children. Children in the US will leave their parents' home when they grow up. They usually live far from their parents because they want to find good jobs. They often write to their parents or telephone them. And they often visit their parents on holidays.

Parents usually let their children choose their own jobs. Americans think it is important for young people to decide on their lives by themselves. Children are asked to do some housework around the house. And in many families, children are paid for doing some housework so that they learn how to make money for their own use.

(   ) 1. Most American families have ______ children.
A. one or two  B. two or three  C. three or four  D. four or five

(   ) 2. American children leave home because ______.
A. their parents are strict  B. they want to find good jobs
C. they don't like their parents  D. they want to travel

(   ) 3. How do children keep in touch with their parents?
A. They write or call.  B. They visit them every day.
C. They send emails only.  D. They don't contact them.

(   ) 4. Who decides on the children's jobs in America?
A. The parents.  B. The teachers.  C. The children themselves.  D. The government.

(   ) 5. Why are children paid for housework in some American families?
A. To make them happy.  B. To teach them to make money.
C. Because parents are too busy.  D. Because they do it very well.

### B. Read and judge T/F 10%

The Shanghai Museum is one of the most famous museums in China. It is in the centre of Shanghai, on People's Square. The museum looks like an ancient Chinese cooking pot. It has eleven galleries and three special exhibition halls.

The museum has a large collection of Chinese art. It has over 120,000 pieces in its collection. You can see ancient bronze works, ceramics, paintings, calligraphy, jade, coins, furniture and many other things. Some of them are over 3,000 years old!

The museum is open from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. every day except Monday. Admission is free, but you need to get a ticket at the gate. Many visitors come from all over the world to see the wonderful collections.

(   ) 1. The Shanghai Museum is on People's Square.
(   ) 2. The museum has 11 special exhibition halls.
(   ) 3. The museum has a collection of over 120,000 items.
(   ) 4. You can see things that are over 3,000 years old.
(   ) 5. The museum is open every day including Monday.

### C. Read and answer questions 10%

My Dream

I have a dream. I want to be a doctor when I grow up. I want to help sick people and make them healthy again.

My mother is a nurse. She works in a big hospital in Shanghai. She often tells me stories about saving people's lives. I think doctors and nurses are very great people. They work hard every day to take care of patients.

To become a doctor, I know I need to study very hard. I must be good at science, especially biology and chemistry. I also need to be kind and patient, because patients need not only medicine but also care and love.

I believe if I work hard, my dream will come true one day.

1. What does the writer want to be? ______
2. Where does the writer's mother work? ______
3. What subjects does the writer need to be good at? ______
4. Besides medicine, what do patients also need? ______
5. What does the writer believe? ______
