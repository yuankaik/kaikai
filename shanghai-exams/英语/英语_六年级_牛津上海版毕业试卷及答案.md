## Part 4 Reading Comprehension（阅读理解） 20%

### 九、Read and judge（阅读短文，判断正误，用T/F表示） 10%

**Passage A: Protecting the Earth**

The Earth is our home. We should protect it. But now people are doing many bad things to the Earth. We cut down too many trees. We use too many plastic bags. We waste too much water. We make the air dirty. These things are bad for the Earth. We should plant more trees. We should use fewer plastic bags. We should save water and energy. We should stop driving cars so much. We can walk or ride bicycles more. If we all care about the Earth, it will be a better place for us to live.

(   ) 1. The Earth is our home.
(   ) 2. People are doing many good things to the Earth now.
(   ) 3. We should use more plastic bags.
(   ) 4. We should save water and energy.
(   ) 5. If we care about the Earth, it will be better.

**Passage B: A Special Birthday**

Last Saturday was my birthday. I turned twelve years old. My parents held a party for me. They invited many of my friends. My mother made a big chocolate cake. It was delicious. My father bought me a new bicycle. It is blue and white — my favourite colours. My friends gave me many presents. Tom gave me a storybook. Alice gave me a beautiful pen. We played games, sang birthday songs, and had a lot of fun. It was a special day. I will never forget it.

(   ) 1. My birthday was on Sunday.
(   ) 2. I turned eleven years old.
(   ) 3. My mother made a big chocolate cake.
(   ) 4. My father bought me a red bicycle.
(   ) 5. Alice gave me a storybook.

### 十、Read and choose（阅读短文，选择正确答案） 10%

**Passage C: Different Ways to School**

In Shanghai, students go to school in different ways. Some students walk to school because they live near the school. Some take the bus or the underground. A few go by car. In my class, most students go to school by bus or on foot. Only five students go by car. I live far from school, so I take the underground every day. It takes me about 30 minutes. I usually read English books on the underground. It is a good way to learn English.

(   ) 1. This passage is about ______.
A. different schools in Shanghai  B. different ways to school  C. different students

(   ) 2. Some students walk to school because ______.
A. they like walking  B. they live near the school  C. their parents ask them to

(   ) 3. In the writer's class, most students go to school ______.
A. by car  B. by underground  C. by bus or on foot

(   ) 4. How does the writer go to school?
A. By bus.  B. By underground.  C. On foot.

(   ) 5. What does the writer do on the way to school?
A. He reads English books.  B. He sleeps.  C. He plays games.

**Passage D: Chinese Festivals**

There are many traditional festivals in China. The Spring Festival is the most important one. It usually comes in January or February. People have a big dinner and set off fireworks. The Dragon Boat Festival is on the fifth day of the fifth lunar month. People eat zongzi and have dragon boat races. The Mid-Autumn Festival is on the 15th day of the eighth lunar month. People eat mooncakes and watch the full moon. All these festivals are very interesting. I like them very much.

(   ) 1. The most important festival in China is ______.
A. the Dragon Boat Festival  B. the Mid-Autumn Festival  C. the Spring Festival

(   ) 2. What do people eat at the Dragon Boat Festival?
A. Dumplings.  B. Zongzi.  C. Mooncakes.

(   ) 3. When is the Mid-Autumn Festival?
A. January or February  B. 5th day of the 5th lunar month  C. 15th day of the 8th lunar month

(   ) 4. What do people do at the Mid-Autumn Festival?
A. Set off fireworks.  B. Have dragon boat races.  C. Watch the full moon.

(   ) 5. How does the writer feel about Chinese festivals?
A. He doesn't like them.  B. He likes them very much.  C. He thinks they are boring.
