### 六、Choose the best answer（选择最恰当的答案） 12%

(   ) 1. —______ will you go this summer holiday? —I will go to Hainan.
A. What  B. Where  C. How

(   ) 2. There ______ a sports meeting in our school next month.
A. will be  B. is  C. will have

(   ) 3. Shanghai is ______ than Beijing.
A. big  B. bigger  C. biggest

(   ) 4. —______ do you visit your grandparents? —Once a month.
A. How long  B. How often  C. How far

(   ) 5. You ______ smoke here. It's not allowed.
A. must  B. can  C. mustn't

(   ) 6. —______ did you go to Beijing? —By plane.
A. How  B. What  C. Where

(   ) 7. Neither my brother nor I ______ a teacher.

(   ) 8. She enjoys ______ to music in her free time.
A. listen  B. listening  C. to listen

(   ) 9. —______ is the weather like today? —It's cloudy and windy.

### 七、Fill in the blanks（用所给词的适当形式填空） 10%

1. Listen! The children _______ (sing) in the music room.
2. She _______ (not watch) TV last night because she was busy.
3. September is the _______ (nine) month of the year.
4. The Changjiang River is the _______ (long) river in China.
5. How many _______ (sheep) can you see on the farm?
6. He _______ (go) to school by bus every day.
7. Would you like _______ (have) some more rice?
8. Look at those _______ (run). They are so fast!
9. My sister is _______ (good) at English than me.
10. If it _______ (not rain) tomorrow, we will go to the park.

### 八、Rewrite the sentences（按要求改写句子，每空一词） 8%

1. She went to Shanghai last week. (改为一般疑问句)
   → _______ she _______ to Shanghai last week?

2. I get up at 6:30 every morning. (对划线部分提问)
   → _______ _______ do you get up every morning?

3. There is some milk in the glass. (改为否定句)
   → There _______ _______ milk in the glass.

4. Tom is the tallest boy in his class. (改为同义句)
   → Tom is _______ _______ any other boy in his class.

5. I spent 50 yuan on this book. (对划线部分提问)
   → _______ _______ did you spend on this book?

## Part 3 Reading Comprehension（阅读理解） 25%

### 九、Read and judge（阅读短文，判断正误，用T/F表示） 15%

**Passage A: A Visit to the Zoo**

Last Sunday, my family and I went to the Shanghai Zoo. The weather was fine. We saw many animals there. The monkeys were very funny. They jumped and climbed everywhere. The elephants were very big. They had long noses and big ears. The pandas were very cute. They ate bamboo leaves. The tigers were strong and walked slowly. My sister was afraid of the snakes. I liked the dolphins best. They were very smart. We had a wonderful time at the zoo.

(   ) 1. We went to the Shanghai Zoo on Saturday.
(   ) 2. The monkeys were very funny.
(   ) 3. The elephants had long noses and small ears.
(   ) 4. My sister liked the snakes.
(   ) 5. I liked the dolphins best.

**Passage B: A Letter to a Pen Pal**

Dear Mary,

My name is Li Hua. I'm twelve years old. I live in Shanghai, a beautiful city in China. I study at Shanghai No.1 Primary School. I'm in Class 2, Grade 5. My favourite subjects are English and science. I like reading books and playing the piano. I have a happy family. There are four people in my family: my father, mother, my little brother and me. My father is a doctor. My mother is a teacher. My brother is only three years old. He is cute. I want to be a scientist when I grow up. Please write to me soon and tell me about yourself.

Yours,
Li Hua

(   ) 1. Li Hua is eleven years old.
(   ) 2. Li Hua lives in Shanghai.
(   ) 3. Li Hua's favourite subject is maths.
(   ) 4. There are five people in Li Hua's family.
(   ) 5. Li Hua wants to be a scientist when he grows up.

**Passage C: Save the Earth**

Our Earth is in danger. The air and water are polluted. Many animals are losing their homes. Forests are becoming smaller and smaller. What can we do to help? First, we should plant more trees. Trees clean the air and give us oxygen. Second, we should use fewer plastic bags. Plastic is bad for the environment. We can use cloth bags instead. Third, we should save water and electricity. Turn off the tap when you brush your teeth. Turn off the light when you leave the room. Fourth, we should recycle things like paper, glass and cans. If we all do these small things, our Earth will become a better place.

(   ) 1. The Earth is healthy now.
(   ) 2. Planting trees can clean the air.
(   ) 3. We should use more plastic bags.
(   ) 4. We should turn off the tap when brushing teeth.
(   ) 5. We should recycle things to help the Earth.

### 十、Read and choose（阅读短文，选择正确答案） 10%

**Passage D: Different Festivals**

Different countries have different festivals. In China, the Spring Festival is the most important. People have a big dinner with their families. They also give red packets to children. In America, people celebrate Thanksgiving Day. They eat turkey and thank people who help them. In England, people like Christmas very much. They put up Christmas trees and give presents to each other. In Thailand, the Water Festival is very popular. People throw water at each other. It's fun! Although festivals are different, they all bring people happiness and joy.

(   ) 1. The most important festival in China is ______.
A. the Spring Festival  B. Christmas  C. the Water Festival

(   ) 2. What do people eat at Thanksgiving in America?
A. Dumplings.  B. Turkey.  C. Chicken.

(   ) 3. What do people do at Christmas in England?
A. They throw water.  B. They put up Christmas trees.  C. They eat zongzi.

(   ) 4. The Water Festival is in ______.
A. China  B. England  C. Thailand

(   ) 5. All festivals bring people ______.
A. money  B. happiness and joy  C. gifts
