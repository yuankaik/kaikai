## 试卷二：小学英语综合期末试卷

### 一、选择题（2×15=30）

1. Tom and Sam come from _________ schools.
   A. same  B. the same  C. such  D. different

2. ______ you do ___________ math?
   A. Do…good at  B. Are…good at  C. Does…well in  D. Do…well in

3. It's time _______ lunch.
   A. to having  B. for having  C. have  D. for have

4. How ______ money does the book ______?
   A. much, cost  B. many, cost  C. much, costs  D. many, costs

## 试卷三：小学英语 Sports Day 期末试卷

### I. 选择题

1. Yesterday was Sports Day. Pat was ____ the long jump and the high jump.
   A. on  B. at  C. in  D. to

2. Lion Team won 20 medals. They won the most medals and they were _____ the first place.
   A. on  B. at  C. in  D. to

3. Mulan was ________ because she was ________ than Cinderella.
   A. better / most exciting  B. better / more exciting
   C. best / most exciting  D. the best / more exciting

4. Tom was _______ than Pat yesterday.
   A. more busy  B. busier  C. busyer  D. more busier

## 试卷四：小学英语语法基础

### 选择题

1. — _______ is your schoolbag?
   — It's on the desk.
   A. What  B. Where  C. Who  D. How

2. I _______ a new friend. Her name _______ Lily.
   A. have; is  B. has; are  C. have; are  D. has; is

3. — Can you _______ English?
   — Yes, I _______.
   A. speak; can  B. say; can  C. speak; do  D. say; do

4. There _______ some milk and two eggs on the table.
   A. is  B. are  C. have  D. has

5. — _______ is the weather like today?
   — It's sunny.
   A. How  B. What  C. Where  D. When
