### 六、Choose the best answer 12%

(   ) 1. —______ will you go this weekend? —To the library.
A. What  B. Where  C. How

(   ) 2. There ______ a sports meeting next Monday.
A. will be  B. is  C. will have

(   ) 3. Which is ______, the sun or the moon?
A. big  B. bigger  C. biggest

(   ) 4. —______ do you visit your grandparents? —Once a month.
A. How long  B. How often  C. How far

(   ) 5. You ______ play football in the street. It's dangerous.
A. must  B. can  C. mustn't

(   ) 6. —______ do you go to school? —On foot.
A. How  B. What  C. Where

(   ) 7. Neither my mother nor my father ______ a teacher.
A. am  B. is  C. are

(   ) 8. I have ______ interesting book. ______ book is about animals.
A. a; The  B. an; The  C. the; An

(   ) 9. —Would you like ______ milk? —Yes, please.
A. some  B. any  C. many

(   ) 10. There ______ some bread and two eggs on the plate.
A. is  B. are  C. have

(   ) 11. She often helps me ______ my English.
A. for  B. with  C. at

(   ) 12. Let's ______ a picnic this Sunday.
A. have  B. having  C. to have

## Part 3 Reading 25%

### A. Read and choose 10%

John is from London. He is now living in Shanghai with his parents. He studies at a primary school in Pudong. He likes his school very much.

John gets up at 6:30 every morning. After breakfast, he goes to school by underground. School starts at 8:00. He has six classes every day. His favourite subject is Chinese because he thinks it's very interesting, though it's a little difficult.

After school, John often plays basketball with his Chinese friends. He can speak some Chinese now. He says, "I love Shanghai. It's a great city. The food here is delicious, especially xiaolongbao. I want to learn more about Chinese culture."

(   ) 1. John is from ______.
A. America  B. England  C. Australia

(   ) 2. How does John go to school?
A. By bus  B. On foot  C. By underground

(   ) 3. How many classes does John have every day?
A. Four  B. Five  C. Six

(   ) 4. What's John's favourite subject?
A. English  B. Chinese  C. Maths

(   ) 5. What does John think of xiaolongbao?
A. It's terrible.  B. It's delicious.  C. It's too sweet.

### B. Read and judge T/F 8%

Shanghai is one of the biggest cities in China. It is in the east of China. The Huangpu River runs through the city. There are many tall buildings in Pudong, such as the Oriental Pearl Tower and the Shanghai Tower. The Bund is a famous place. You can see many old buildings there. Many visitors come to Shanghai every year.

(   ) 1. Shanghai is in the west of China.
(   ) 2. The Huangpu River runs through Shanghai.
(   ) 3. There are many short buildings in Pudong.
(   ) 4. The Bund is a famous place in Shanghai.

### C. Read and answer 7%

My name is Amy. I'm a student at Shanghai Primary School. I'm in Grade 5. My birthday is on October 8th. I'm going to have a birthday party at home. I will invite my best friends. My mother will make a big cake for me. My father will buy some balloons. I'm very excited!

1. What grade is Amy in? ______
2. When is Amy's birthday? ______
3. Where is Amy going to have her birthday party? ______
4. What will Amy's mother do? ______
5. How does Amy feel? ______
