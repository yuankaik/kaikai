### 十、Read the passage and judge T/F（阅读判断） 12%

Hello, my name is Lily. I am eight years old. I am a student at Fushan Foreign Language Primary School. I am in Class 2, Grade 3.

I get up at 6:30 every morning. I have breakfast at 7:00. I like bread and milk for breakfast. Then I go to school at 7:30. I go to school on foot because my home is near the school.

I have four classes in the morning. My favourite subject is English. My English teacher is Miss Wang. She is very kind and beautiful. I also like Art because I like drawing.

After school, I go home and do my homework. Then I play with my little dog, Bobby. He is white and very cute. I love him very much.

(   ) 1. Lily is nine years old.
(   ) 2. Lily studies at Fushan Foreign Language Primary School.
(   ) 3. Lily has breakfast at 7:30.
(   ) 4. Lily goes to school by bus.
(   ) 5. Lily's English teacher is Miss Wang.
(   ) 6. Lily has a white cat.
