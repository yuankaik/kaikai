### 九、Read and judge（阅读短文，判断正误，用T/F表示） 10%

**Passage A**

I have four classes in the morning and two classes in the afternoon. My favourite subject is English. After school, I often play basketball with my friends. I have dinner at 6:00 in the evening. After dinner, I do my homework and read books. I go to bed at 9:00.

(   ) 2. Jack's school is far from his home.  
(   ) 3. Jack has six classes a day.  
(   ) 4. Jack's favourite subject is English.  
(   ) 5. Jack goes to bed at 9:00.
