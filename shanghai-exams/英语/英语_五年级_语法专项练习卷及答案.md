## 六、时态专项（Choose the correct answer） 15%

## 七、句型转换（Rewrite the sentences） 15%

1. She does her homework every day.（改为否定句）
   She ______ ______ her homework every day.

2. There is some milk in the glass.（改为一般疑问句）
   ______ there ______ milk in the glass?

3. He goes to school by bus.（对划线部分提问）
   ______ ______ he go to school?

4. They are playing basketball now.（对划线部分提问）
   ______ are they ______ now?

5. The book costs 20 yuan.（对划线部分提问）
   ______ ______ does the book cost?

6. She is a lovely girl.（改为感叹句）
   ______ a lovely girl she is!

7. He is too young to go to school.（改为同义句）
   He is ______ young ______ he can't go to school.

8. I went to the park yesterday.（改为一般疑问句）
   ______ you ______ to the park yesterday?

9. They will visit the museum next Sunday.（改为否定句）
   They ______ ______ the museum next Sunday.

10. She has already finished her work.（改为否定句）
    She ______ finished her work ______.
