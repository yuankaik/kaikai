## Part 3 Reading Comprehension（阅读理解） 20%

### 九、Read and judge（阅读短文，判断正误，用T/F表示） 10%

**A Picnic Day**

Today is Saturday. The weather is fine. My family and I are having a picnic in the park. There are many people in the park. Some children are flying kites. Some old people are doing tai chi. My father is reading a book under a big tree. My mother is preparing the food. I'm playing with my little sister. We have sandwiches, fruit, and juice for our picnic. The sandwiches are delicious. We are all very happy.

(   ) 1. It's Sunday today.  
(   ) 2. The weather is not good.  
(   ) 3. Some old people are doing tai chi in the park.  
(   ) 4. My father is sleeping under a tree.  
(   ) 5. We have sandwiches, fruit, and juice for our picnic.  

**Animal School**

There is an Animal School in the forest. Mr. Owl is the teacher. There are many animal students in the school. Little Rabbit likes English. Little Monkey likes maths. Little Bear likes PE. They all study hard. There is a big playground in Animal School. The animals like to play games there after class. There are many classrooms too. The animals like their school very much.

(   ) 1. There are no classrooms in Animal School.  
(   ) 2. Mr. Owl is the teacher in Animal School.  
(   ) 3. Little Rabbit likes maths.  
(   ) 4. The animals like to play games in the playground.  
(   ) 5. The animals don't like their school.  

### 十、Read and choose（阅读短文，选择正确答案） 10%

**My Best Friend**

My best friend is Lucy. She is ten years old. She is tall and thin. She has big eyes and long hair. She studies at Sunshine Primary School. She is in Class 2, Grade 4. Her favourite subject is English. She can speak English very well. She likes reading English storybooks. After school, she often plays the piano. She can play it beautifully. She wants to be an English teacher in the future. I like her very much.

(   ) 1. Lucy is _______ years old.  
A. nine  B. ten  C. eleven  

(   ) 2. Lucy is _______.  
A. short and thin  B. tall and thin  C. tall and fat  

(   ) 3. Lucy is in _______.  
A. Class 4, Grade 2  B. Class 2, Grade 4  C. Class 2, Grade 5  

(   ) 4. Lucy's favourite subject is _______.  
A. maths  B. English  C. music  

(   ) 5. Lucy wants to be _______ in the future.  
A. a doctor  B. a pianist  C. an English teacher  

**Four Seasons**

There are four seasons in a year. They are spring, summer, autumn, and winter. Spring is warm and green. Flowers bloom in spring. Summer is hot. People like to go swimming in summer. Autumn is cool and golden. Leaves fall from the trees. Winter is cold and white. It often snows in winter. Children like to make snowmen and have snowball fights. Each season is beautiful in its own way.

(   ) 1. How many seasons are there in a year?  
A. Three.  B. Four.  C. Five.  

(   ) 2. What's the weather like in spring?  
A. It's hot.  B. It's warm.  C. It's cold.  

(   ) 3. What do people like to do in summer?  
A. Go swimming.  B. Make snowmen.  C. Fly kites.  

(   ) 4. What happens in autumn?  
A. Flowers bloom.  B. It snows.  C. Leaves fall from the trees.  

(   ) 5. What do children like to do in winter?  
A. Go swimming.  B. Make snowmen.  C. Plant trees.
