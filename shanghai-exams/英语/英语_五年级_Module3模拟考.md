# 上海小学英语 - 五年级 Module 3 模拟考
## Oxford Shanghai Edition 5A Module 3 Places and activities

**时间：50分钟  满分：100分**

### Part 1 Listening 35分

**I. Listen and choose（听音选词）8分**
1. A. supermarket  B. cinema  C. hospital
2. A. turn left  B. turn right  C. go straight
3. A. museum  B. library  C. park
4. A. by bus  B. by car  C. on foot

**II. Listen and order（听音排序）8分**
(   ) Go along this street.
(   ) Turn right at the second crossing.
(   ) The cinema is on your left.
(   ) You can't miss it.

**III. Listen and choose the answer（听问句选答）9分**
1. A. It's near the park.  B. Go straight.  C. Yes, it is.
2. A. By bus.  B. At 8:00.  C. With my friend.
3. A. Yes, there is.  B. Yes, it is.  C. No, I don't.

**IV. Listen and fill（听音填空）10分**
A: Excuse me, how can I get to the (1)______?
B: Go (2)______ this street. Turn (3)______ at the first crossing. You will see it on your (4)______.
A: Is it far from here?
B: No, it's about (5)______ minutes' walk.

### Part 2 Written 65分

**I. Read and choose（选出不同类）10分**
1. A. cinema  B. hospital  C. street  D. museum
2. A. left  B. right  C. straight  D. walk
3. A. bus  B. car  C. bike  D. way

**II. Multiple choice（选择）20分**
1. — How can I (    ) the museum? — Turn left at the corner.
   A. get  B. get to  C. go
2. The hospital is (    ) the left.
   A. in  B. at  C. on
3. — (    ) is the cinema? — It's about 10 minutes' walk.
   A. How far  B. How long  C. How much

**III. Complete the dialogue（补全对话）15分**
A: Excuse me, (1)______ can I get to the Shanghai Museum?
B: You can (2)______ Bus No. 49.
A: Where should I (3)______ off?
B: (4)______ off at People's Square. The museum is (5)______ to the square.

**IV. Reading comprehension（阅读理解）20分**
Shanghai is a big city. There are many interesting places. The Bund is famous. You can see many old buildings there. Shanghai Museum is near People's Square. It has many old things. If you like shopping, you can go to Nanjing Road. It is a busy street with many shops.
1. Shanghai is a (    ) city. (big/small)
2. The Bund has many (    ) buildings.
3. Shanghai Museum is near (    ).
4. Nanjing Road is a (    ) street.
5. You can go (    ) on Nanjing Road.
