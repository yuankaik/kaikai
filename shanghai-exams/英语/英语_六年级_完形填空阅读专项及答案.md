## Part A 完形填空（Cloze Test） 50%

### Cloze 1 — My New School (15%)

I am a new student at Shanghai No. 1 Middle School. I (1) ______ to this school last month. At first, I was a little (2) ______ because I didn't know anyone. But my classmates are very (3) ______. They helped me a lot.

Our school is very big and beautiful. There (4) ______ about 50 classrooms and a big library. The library has (5) ______ of books. I like reading there after school.

My favourite subject is science. Our science teacher, Mr Li, makes the classes very (6) ______. We do many experiments in the lab. Last week, we (7) ______ how plants grow. It was amazing!

I also joined the basketball club. I practice basketball (8) ______ a week, on Tuesdays and Fridays. I have made many new friends in the club. I (9) ______ my school life very much. I'm sure I (10) ______ a great time here.

(   ) 1. A. come  B. came  C. coming  D. comes
(   ) 2. A. happy  B. excited  C. nervous  D. angry
(   ) 3. A. strict  B. friendly  C. lazy  D. quiet
(   ) 4. A. is  B. are  C. was  D. were
(   ) 5. A. thousand  B. thousands  C. a thousand  D. the thousand
(   ) 6. A. boring  B. difficult  C. interesting  D. easy
(   ) 7. A. learn  B. learned  C. will learn  D. are learning
(   ) 8. A. once  B. two  C. twice  D. second
(   ) 9. A. like  B. likes  C. liked  D. will like

## Part B 阅读理解（Reading Comprehension） 50%

### Passage 1 — A Visit to the Doctor (12%)

It was Monday morning. Tom didn't feel well. He had a headache and a fever. His mother took him to the hospital.

At the hospital, a nurse took his temperature first. It was 38.5°C. Then the doctor came and asked Tom some questions. "Do you have a cough?" "No." "Do you have a stomach ache?" "No." "What did you eat yesterday?" "I ate a lot of ice cream."

The doctor smiled and said, "You ate too much ice cream. That's why you're sick. You should drink more warm water and have a good rest. Don't eat too much cold food." The doctor gave Tom some medicine. Tom promised he would be more careful next time.

(   ) 1. What was wrong with Tom?
A. He had a cough.  B. He had a headache and fever.  C. He had a stomach ache.

(   ) 2. What was Tom's temperature?
A. 37.5°C  B. 38.5°C  C. 39.5°C

(   ) 3. Why was Tom sick?
A. He ate too much rice.  B. He ran too much.  C. He ate too much ice cream.

(   ) 4. What did the doctor tell Tom to do?
A. Eat more ice cream.  B. Drink warm water and rest.  C. Go to school.

(   ) 5. What did the doctor give Tom?
A. Some food.  B. Some medicine.  C. Some money.

(   ) 6. What did Tom promise?
A. To eat more ice cream.  B. To be more careful.  C. To study harder.

### Passage 2 — Four Great Inventions of Ancient China (12%)

China has a long history. The four great inventions of ancient China are papermaking, printing, gunpowder and the compass. These inventions changed the world.

Papermaking was invented by Cai Lun in the Eastern Han Dynasty (about 2,000 years ago). Before paper, people wrote on bamboo or silk. Paper was much cheaper and lighter.

Printing made it possible to produce many books quickly. Bi Sheng invented movable type printing in the Song Dynasty.

Gunpowder was first invented by accident when ancient Chinese people were trying to make medicine. Later it was used in fireworks and weapons.

The compass helped people find directions at sea. It was a simple but very useful tool. It made long sea journeys possible.

(   ) 1. How many great inventions are mentioned in the passage?
A. Three  B. Four  C. Five

(   ) 2. Who invented papermaking?
A. Bi Sheng  B. Cai Lun  C. We don't know

(   ) 3. When was papermaking invented?
A. About 1,000 years ago.  B. About 2,000 years ago.  C. About 3,000 years ago.

(   ) 4. What were people trying to make when they invented gunpowder?
A. Fireworks  B. Weapons  C. Medicine

(   ) 5. What was the compass used for?
A. Finding directions  B. Printing books  C. Making paper

(   ) 6. Which invention made long sea journeys possible?
A. Papermaking  B. Gunpowder  C. The compass

### Passage 3 — A Special Day (14%)

Last Friday was a very special day for our class. We had an English Day at school. Everyone had to speak only English for the whole day!

In the morning, we had an English assembly. Our headmaster gave a speech in English. Then some students performed an English play "The Three Little Pigs". It was very funny. Everyone laughed.

At lunchtime, we had English menus in the canteen. We had to order food in English. "I'd like some rice and chicken, please." It was a little difficult but fun.

In the afternoon, we had an English quiz competition. Our team answered 8 out of 10 questions correctly and won second prize! We got an English storybook each as a prize.

Before going home, our English teacher Miss Chen said, "You all did a great job today. I'm proud of you." I felt very happy. I hope we can have another English Day soon!

(   ) 1. When was the English Day?
A. Last Monday  B. Last Friday  C. Last Wednesday

(   ) 2. What play did the students perform?
A. "Cinderella"  B. "The Three Little Pigs"  C. "Snow White"

(   ) 3. Where did they have English menus?
A. In the classroom  B. In the library  C. In the canteen

(   ) 4. What prize did the writer's team win?
A. First prize  B. Second prize  C. Third prize

(   ) 5. What did they get as a prize?
A. A toy  B. A storybook  C. Some money

(   ) 6. Who is Miss Chen?
A. The headmaster  B. The English teacher  C. A student

(   ) 7. How did the writer feel at the end of the day?
A. Sad  B. Tired  C. Happy

### Passage 4 — The Ant and the Grasshopper (12%)

In a field one summer's day, a grasshopper was hopping about, chirping and singing happily. An ant passed by, carrying a big piece of corn to his nest.

"Why not come and chat with me," said the grasshopper, "instead of working so hard?"

"I am helping to store food for the winter," said the ant, "and I think you should do the same."

"Why worry about winter?" said the grasshopper. "We have plenty of food now." But the ant went on its way and continued its work.

When the winter came, the grasshopper had no food and found itself hungry and cold, while it saw the ants eating corn from the stores they had collected in the summer.

Then the grasshopper knew: it is best to prepare for the days of need.

(   ) 1. What was the grasshopper doing in summer?
A. Working hard  B. Singing and playing  C. Collecting food

(   ) 2. What was the ant doing?
A. Playing  B. Singing  C. Carrying food to its nest

(   ) 3. What did the ant tell the grasshopper to do?
A. To sing more  B. To store food for winter  C. To go away

(   ) 4. What happened to the grasshopper in winter?
A. It had plenty of food.  B. It was hungry and cold.  C. It went to the ant's house.

(   ) 5. What did the grasshopper learn?
A. Summer is fun.  B. Ants are not friendly.  C. It's best to prepare for the future.

(   ) 6. What is the moral of this story?
A. Work hard and prepare for the future.  B. Enjoy life while you can.
C. Never trust ants.
