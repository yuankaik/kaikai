> 来源：上海小学二年级英语期中考试综合整理（道客巴巴/原创力/21世纪教育网）
> 完卷时间：40分钟  满分：100分

---

## Part 1 Listening（听力部分） 50%

### 一、Listen and circle the picture you hear（听录音，圈出正确的图片） 12%

1. A. cat  B. dog  C. bird
2. A. apple  B. banana  C. orange
3. A. sunny  B. rainy  C. cloudy
4. A. red  B. blue  C. yellow
5. A. spring  B. summer  C. autumn
6. A. milk  B. water  C. juice

### 二、Listen and tick the correct sentence（听录音，在正确的句子旁打√） 10%

(   ) 1. A. I like spring.  B. I like summer.
(   ) 2. A. It's a sunny day.  B. It's a rainy day.
(   ) 3. A. She can sing.  B. She can dance.
(   ) 4. A. I have a cat.  B. I have a dog.
(   ) 5. A. The book is on the desk.  B. The book is under the desk.

### 三、Listen and number the pictures（听录音，给图片排序1-5） 10%

(   ) eating  (   ) sleeping  (   ) running  (   ) reading  (   ) singing

### 四、Listen and choose the correct response（听问句，选答句） 8%

(   ) 1. A. I'm fine.  B. I'm seven.  C. I'm Tom.
(   ) 2. A. It's a book.  B. It's red.  C. It's big.
(   ) 3. A. Yes, I am.  B. Yes, I do.  C. Yes, I can.
(   ) 4. A. There are three.  B. They are red.  C. I have three.

### 五、Listen and colour（听录音，涂颜色） 10%

2. The banana is _______.
3. The sky is _______.
4. The cat is _______.
5. The flower is _______.

### 八、Choose the correct word（选择正确的单词填空） 10%

(   ) 1. —What ______ is it? —It's red.
A. colour  B. animal  C. food

(   ) 2. I ______ a boy. My name ______ Tom.
A. am; is  B. is; am  C. are; is

(   ) 3. —______ you like milk? —Yes, I do.
A. Can  B. Do  C. Are

(   ) 4. The cat is ______ the box.
A. on  B. at  C. to

(   ) 5. There ______ two apples on the table.
A. is  B. am  C. are

(   ) 6. —______ is he? —He is my father.
A. What  B. Who  C. Where

(   ) 7. I can ______ a bike.
A. ride  B. rides  C. riding

(   ) 8. —How many ______ do you have? —I have three.
A. pen  B. a pen  C. pens

(   ) 9. She is ______ girl.
A. a  B. an  C. /

(   ) 10. The rabbit has two long ______.
A. ear  B. ears  C. a ear

### 九、Read and choose T/F（阅读并判断） 10%

Hello! I'm Peter. I am seven years old. I am a student. I like animals very much. I have a dog. Its name is Doudou. It is brown and white. It has big eyes and a short tail. It likes to play with a ball.

I also have a cat. Its name is Mimi. Mimi is white. It has blue eyes. It likes to sleep on the sofa. I like my pets very much.

(   ) 1. Peter is eight years old.
(   ) 2. Peter has a dog and a cat.
(   ) 3. The dog's name is Mimi.
(   ) 4. The dog has a long tail.
(   ) 5. The cat likes to sleep on the sofa.
